Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

About Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, also known as the Father of the Nation in India, was a leader of non-violent resistance against British rule. His principles of truth and non-violence (Satyagraha) inspired movements for civil rights and freedom worldwide.

Why I Admire Mahatma Gandhi

  • Champion of non-violence and peace
  • Fought for India's independence
  • Inspired civil rights movements worldwide
  • Lived a life of simplicity and truth
